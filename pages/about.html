About Afriski Winter Festival

The Afriski Winter Adventure Festival widely known as Winterfest is the flagship event of the Afriski Mountain Resort, located 3,222 meters above sea level in Lesotho’s Maloti Mountains.The festival as we know it today began to take shape over a decade ago. It was designed to bridge the gap between hard-core snow sports and the vibrant South African music scene. By 2015, it was officially billed as "Africa’s highest altitude music festival.The event has expanded significantly, partnering with major sponsors like Jacaranda FM, Maluti Premium Lager, and Econet Lesotho. It now attracts thousands of "Winterfesters" who come for the music as much as the slopes.

Why this festival is important

  1. Promotes tourism in Lesotho
  2. Supports local businesses
  3. Showcases Basotho culture
  4. Encourages adventure tourism

Target Tourists

  • Adventure tourists
  • Ski and snowboard lovers
  • International travellers
  • Families and holiday visitors
